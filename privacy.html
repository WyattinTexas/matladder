Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the following apps published by Corkscrew Games LLC:

  • FOOTWORK - Drill Timer (com.corkscrewgames.footwork)
  • Quiet Eye (com.corkscrewgames.quieteye)

Data We Collect

Our apps are designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Name or Nickname: You may optionally enter a display name. This is stored locally on your device and, if you join a group leaderboard, shared with other members of that group.
  • Drill Times / Scores: Your performance data (times, scores, streaks) is stored locally on your device using browser storage (localStorage).
  • Group Leaderboard Data: If you join a group in FOOTWORK, your name and drill times are sent to Firebase Realtime Database so other group members can see the leaderboard. No account or email is required.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Payment information
  • Health or biometric data
  • Browsing history
  • Contacts or photos

Third-Party Services

Our apps use Google Firebase Realtime Database solely for optional group leaderboard functionality. Firebase may collect standard technical data (IP addresses, device type) as described in Google's Privacy Policy. We do not use Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, or any other Firebase services.

Our apps do not contain advertisements or third-party tracking SDKs.

Data Storage

All personal performance data is stored locally on your device. Group leaderboard data is stored on Firebase servers in the United States. You can clear your local data at any time by clearing your app's storage in device settings.

Children's Privacy

Our apps do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The apps are suitable for all ages and do not require account creation.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
